Objectives

The project's general objective is to design a prototype that will improve the survival rate of the Pacific white-leg shrimp through water quality manipulation using behavioral detection of a shrimp. Also, to develop a prototype that controls and monitors the water quality of the Shrimp fry hatchery, develop an android application that displays water parameters and regulates controlled devices, and test and evaluate the efficiency of the project.
