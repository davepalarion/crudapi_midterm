Introduction

The project is a controlled microcontroller-based system that can measure and control the salinity, pH, Temperature, and oxygen level based on behavioral detection in a controlled environment. The sensors' measured water parameters such as salinity, pH, Temperature, and oxygen goes into the microcontroller as the input. The system equilibrates the water quality if it senses that it is below or above the standard value. It will do so until the common ideal measurements are achieved.
